Introduction

ONER (kunyin sizi), a singing group in mainland China, is composed of Pinkray,Kwin and Didi.In 2017, as a BC221 trainee, the group released their first song "if you can feel my feeling". In January 2018, they participated in the iQIYI idol competition show "idol producer", and all the members qualified for the top 35. On August 30, the group released its first album, "allergy", which was the official debut. On November 3, they won the award of the most popular group in the annual grand ceremony of the new sound power assembly of the popular music chart. On June 14, 2019, the group released their second album "PROM".
